Instructions:

  • To add a new annotation: select a block of text, enter the note and tags, and press "annotate". Multiple tags can be inputted with a comma separator.
  • Mousing over an annotation will reveal the note.
  • Clicking on a tag will highlight any annotations containing that tag in orange.

Known Deficiencies:

  • You can't make 2 overlapping highlights
  • You can't delete highlights once they've been saved
